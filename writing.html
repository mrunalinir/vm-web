SHERLOCKED

Event Overview

A gruesome murder has occurred on the campus of BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus and this is a shoutout to all investigators to help find the perpetrator. You will be provided with a detailed report by the local police and a CBI report of the suspects with their respective character sketches and relation to the deceased. You will also have the liberty to interview suspects and, if needed, to bend them till they break.

Rules of Procedure

  • Use your deductive skills to find out who the murderer is.
  • Provide a detailed report mentioning the motive behind the crime, and the means and opportunity the killer took advantage of.
  • Give a coherent final conclusion.

Yin Yang

Event Overview

There could be multiple sides to a story depending on whose shoe you are in. What this event asks for, however, is just one complementary pair. Yin Yang, like the Chinese concept itself, requires you to view a situation in contrasting, yet complementary, perspectives, and weave a plot worth the prize.

Rules of Procedure

  • Participate in teams of two .
  • Write the yin or yang version of a prompt that suits your liking and swap with your partner.
  • Write a complementary (not necessarily opposite) version for whatever your partner has written

Rise Through The Ages

Event Overview

The idea of what a revolution constitutes has changed and will continue to change over the years. After all, the canvas upon which revolutionary ideas, wars and people are painted changes with society. There are always new problems or old traditions, decisions that should be remade, people rediscovering the meaning of being human.

And in that spirit, this Verba Maximus presents its first theme event wherein the writer must choose an age and reconstruct the world during particular revolution in a short story, poem or any other literary means, from the eyes of those involved. The writer must bring out the all various aspects of how revolutions change people, society, commerce etc - small or big.

Rules of Procedure

  • Entries are allowed only in the English language.
  • There is no word limit.
  • Entries will be judged on the basis of creativity, relevance to the prompt, style and overall coherence.

Quill the Revolution

Event Overview

Draw inspiration from your favourite fictional universes and write a fan-fic or a what if story which follows a revolutionary character, idea or pov. Describe the world after the Thanos snap, or under the rule of Lord Voldemort, or just rewrite your favourite song from les miserables! The possibilities are endless, so put your world building caps on and re-imagine your favourite book, movie or game from ashes to glory.

Rules of Procedure

  • Submit your short story, prose or poem (in english only) to verbamaximus@gmail.com
  • Word limit is up to 5000 words.
  • Submit your entries by the 18th of January.
  • Plagiarism of any form will not be accepted.
